Community Outreach

At Flight 13, we believe in giving back to our community through various outreach programs and initiatives.

Charity Events

Annual Basketball for Charity tournament raising funds for local youth programs. Last year we raised over $15,000 for community centers.

School Visits

Our players regularly visit local schools to inspire young athletes and promote the importance of education and sports.

Volunteer Work

Monthly community service activities including park cleanups, food drives, and coaching clinics for underprivileged youth.
